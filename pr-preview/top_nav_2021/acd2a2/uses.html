The Best Way To Move Money Around the World

Using XRP

XRP and the XRP Ledger are used to power innovative technology across the payments space.

From cross-border payments to decentralized finance, businesses and developers around the world are leveraging the XRP Ledger and its native digital asset, XRP—a faster, cheaper and greener currency.

Cross-Border Payments

Cross-Border Payments

Through RippleNet, financial institutions can use XRP to bridge two currencies in 3-5 seconds, ensuring payments are quickly sent and received in the local currency on either side of a transaction.

Learn About On-Demand Liquidity
Micropayments

Micropayments

Businesses are leveraging XRP and the XRP Ledger to build innovative products for gaming, content and web monetization, among other applications where money is at the center.

Learn About XRP Ledger Businesses
Digital Cryptocurrency

Digital Cryptocurrency Wallets

Individuals can use mobile wallets to store private and public passwords and interact with various blockchains to send and receive digital assets, including XRP.

Explore Wallets
Exchanges

Exchanges

Market makers and individuals use exchanges to trade XRP and other digital assets. Exchanges are often also intermediaries in cross-border payments among financial institutions.

Explore Exchanges
Trading

Institutional Trading

Market participants use XRP as a high-speed, cost-efficient and reliable trading collateral. This means seizing arbitrage opportunities, servicing margin calls and managing general trade inventory in real time.
