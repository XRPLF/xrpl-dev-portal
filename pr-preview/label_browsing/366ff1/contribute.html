XRP Ledger Standards

Thank you for your interest in contributing to XRPL.org. This website was created for the community and is meant to be a living, breathing source of truth for XRP and XRP Ledger resources.

Contributing to XRPL.org is a great way to learn about the XRP Ledger. This portal is open-source and anyone can suggest changes. For more information, please read and consider the contributing guidelines.

You may also be interested in learning about Interledger, RippleX, and PayString.
